# AI Usage Statement

This project made use of AI-assisted tools in the following capacities, all under the critical review and judgement of the author.

ChatGPT and Claude were used to support the planning of the project structure and to assist with debugging during software development. Both tools were also consulted when working with the Mesa agent-based modelling framework and its documentation, helping to interpret and navigate unfamiliar API interfaces during the simulation development phase.

In all cases, outputs from these tools were critically evaluated by the author before being used or incorporated. No AI tool was used to generate the research question, experimental design, analysis, results, or report content. All intellectual contributions, design decisions, and interpretations are the author's own.
